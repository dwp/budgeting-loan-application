If you think the decision you’ve been given is wrong

Write to the Social Fund by 2 April 2020 explaining why you think the decision is wrong. If you send your letter after this date, you’ll need to tell us why it’s late.

You or your appointee, if you have one, must sign the letter. Include your National Insurance number.

You should get a reply within 14 days.
