Your Budgeting Loan application decision

You applied for a Budgeting Loan of £300 on 6 December 2019.

We can offer you the £300 you asked for.

You have until 12 January 2020 to decide whether to accept the loan.

If you accept the loan

You’ll receive the money within 7 days. The loan will be paid into the account that your benefit is paid into.

Repayments will be taken automatically from your benefits. This means that your benefit payment will reduce from £56.50 to £50 for 37 weeks.

You’ll still have to pay back the loan if you stop claiming benefits.

How this is worked out

This is based on:

  • your income, including any benefits you receive
  • whether you’re paying back any other Budgeting Loans or Crisis Loans
  • any other debts you have

If you’re not happy with the decision

You can ask us to review a decision. To do this, write to the Social Fund before 12 January 2020 explaining why you think the decision is wrong.
