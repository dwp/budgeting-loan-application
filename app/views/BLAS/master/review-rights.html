Contact the Social Fund

You can talk to an adviser about the decision we have given you.

  • Social Fund
  • Telephone: 0800 169 0140
  • Textphone: 0800 169 286
  • Welsh language: 0800 169 0240
  • Relay UK (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0800 169 0140
  • Video relay service for British Sign Language (BSL) users - check you can use the service
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm
  • Find out about call charges

If you would like us to review the decision

Write to the Social Fund within one month of the decision (the day we contacted you) explaining why you think our decision is wrong. If you send your letter after this date, you will need to tell us why it is late.

You or your appointee, if you have one, must sign the letter. Include your National Insurance number.

Staff will review the decision. You should get a reply within 14 days.
