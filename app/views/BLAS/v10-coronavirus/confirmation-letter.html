Application received

What happens next

You’ll get a decision by post within 20 working days (4 weeks).

If you’re offered a loan

You’ll be told:

  • how much you can borrow
  • how much your repayments will be if you accept the loan
Budgeting Loan repayments have been temporarily stopped because of coronavirus (COVID-19).

If choose to accept the loan you must sign page 4 (the offer letter) as well as page 5 (the reply slip).

You do not have to accept the loan offer if you do not want to.

If you’re not offered a loan

You’ll be told why and how to get help if you need it.
