What type of Employment and Support Allowance do you get?

Contact Jobcentre Plus to find out what type of Employment and Support Allowance you get.

If you’ve had a Budgeting Loan before and your benefits haven’t changed, you’ll probably be getting income-related Employment and Support Allowance.

If you get contribution-based Employment and Support Allowance, you won’t be able to get a Budgeting Loan.
