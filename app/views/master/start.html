Apply

If you get Universal Credit you cannot get a Budgeting Loan. Apply for a Budgeting Advance instead.

If you do not get Universal Credit check if you're eligible for a Budgeting Loan.

If you are eligible, you can apply online or using the paper form. It’s quicker to apply online.

There’s a different way to apply in Northern Ireland.

Apply online for a Budgeting Loan

If you apply online

If you can receive text messages or email, you could have a decision within 7 days. Once your application is processed, we will tell you how to view your decision online.

If you accept a loan offer, you'll receive the money within 7 days. It will be paid into your bank, building society or credit union account.

If you apply using the paper form

If you apply by post you will receive a decision within 31 days.

You will need to fill in form SF500. You can either:

  • download and print the form
  • pick it up from your nearest Jobcentre Plus
  • ask the Social Fund to post it to you - allow 7 days for the form to arrive

Social Fund
Telephone: 0800 169 0140
Welsh language: 0800 169 0240
NGT text relay (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0800 169 0140
Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm
Find out about call charges


If you want to accept the loan you must sign page 4 of the acceptance letter and return it in the pre-paid envelope provided.

Return it to the address on the letter. Do not send it to your local jobcentre as this may delay your loan.

Make sure the return address is fully visible in the envelope window.

You will then receive the money within 25 days. It will be paid into your bank, building society or credit union account.

Questions about your application

Call the Social Fund if you have a question about the progress of your application.

Wait 14 days before phoning if you applied online.

Wait 21 days before phoning if you applied by post.
