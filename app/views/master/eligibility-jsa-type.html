What type of Jobseeker's Allowance do you get?

Contact Jobcentre Plus to find out what type of Jobseeker’s Allowance you get.

If you’ve had a Budgeting Loan before and your benefits haven’t changed, you’ll probably be getting income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance.

If you get contribution-based Jobseeker's Allowance, you won’t be able to get a Budgeting Loan.
